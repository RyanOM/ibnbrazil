About IBN Brazil

It's Our Business to help your Business

We are a group of Irish individuals focused on developing Irish and Brazilian business relations. With a diverse and experienced background, we aim to:

  • Be the representative voice of Irish Business in Brazil
  • Support Brazilian companies which have invested in or trade with Ireland
  • Provide links to Business Networks and Chambers in Ireland and in Brazil
  • Forge stronger commercial ties between Ireland and Brazil

Our Services

Connecting

We provide the opportunity for members to meet people in other business sectors and industries.

Promoting

Trade and investment between Brazil and Ireland.

Supporting

We offer market entry strategies for Irish companies looking to establish in Brazil.

Learning

We provide members with opportunities to learn about other business sectors and industries.

Access

We provide access directly or through referrals to key contacts and potential decision-makers in different business sectors .
