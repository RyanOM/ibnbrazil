Technology Trends in Brazil

7:00pm, Thursday 13th June 2019

NEX RIO - Ladeira da Glória, 26 - Glória, Rio de Janeiro

Technology Trends in Brazil

An evening of Discussion with the local Irish and Brazilian Business Communities

At a time of economic uncertainty for Brazil, technology is a potential growth area. We gather digital innovators and technology professionals, fast-growing start ups, futurists and heads of industry to ask a simple question: where to next for Brazil?

This event will encourage innovative thinking about digital transformation and the opportunities presented by technology.

We will connect digital professionals, companies, public and private institutions offering an informative and interactive experience.

Speakers

Luís dos Santos Durão

Co-Founder at Lumen Data Intelligence

Pitch

The next few years will witness a transformation of social relations by artificial intelligence. Allowed by the constant advancement of data storage and data processing technologies, the digital revolution encompasses the integration between the physical and the digital world. Just in the production and consumption fields, for example, the development of intelligent systems through algorithms guide decision-making processes, lead the development of new products, and unravel patterns and consumption habits.

Thus, artificial intelligence is an important instrument for transforming social relations through 'thinking' systems, and uses data and computational techniques to achieve greater effectiveness.

Jean-Christophe Bonis

Founder at Oxymore Inc

Pitch

Our societies are deeply changing, through exponential acceleration of technological progress. One of the main issue of our time is our personal data and its treatment How do we accept to trade it, in exchange of what services? What private companies and States do with our data?

Our way of interacting with each other, and even our brains, are beginning to change under the effect of screens. If technology makes our lives easier, how to set boundaries between real life and virtuality?

We have the opportunity to reshape our society, in a 4.0 version. How do we want it and what will we pass on to the young generations?
