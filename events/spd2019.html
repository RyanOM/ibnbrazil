Ceann Comhairle Event

Deepening the links between Ireland & Brazil

5:30pm 21st March, 2019

Ireland House, Alameda Santos, 787, São Paulo

New Business Horizons
for Brazil and Ireland

An evening of Discussion with the local Irish and Brazilian Business Communities

Few countries in the world have grown trade links in relative terms as quickly as Ireland and Brazil over the past 10 years. This growth in trade is a recognition that both countries have so much to offer each other and how we’ve still only achieved a fraction of what’s possible.

This meeting is a celebration of the flourishing relationship that’s happening right now between the Irish and Brazilians - in business, culture and elsewhere.

Above all, it’s an opportunity for both communities to get together and enjoy an evening of discussion in the presence of Ireland’s Ceann Comhairle and some sociable networking afterwards.

Speaker

Seán Ó Fearghaíl

Presidente da Câmara de Deputados da Irlanda

Seán Ó Fearghaíl is currently Ireland’s Ceann Comhairle, a position he has held since March 2016. This is his first visit to Brazil, where he will visit Rio de Janeiro, Brasilia and São Paulo.

As someone who was previously spokesperson for his party on Foreign Affairs and Trade, Seán is acutely aware of the importance of developing links between Brazil and Ireland at every level.

Everyone is invited to participate, bring their own questions and ideas, and generally get involved. In his role as Ceann Comhairle, Mr. Ó Fearghaíl is extremely accustomed to chairing a lively discussion!
